I didn't really understand the assignment, but I will do my best to describe my plan. The site name will be Adam's T's. Kinda quirky and enough for a school project. The purpose will be to help others customize and order their own tshirts. The main questions that will be answered are what size should be the tshirts, what color should be the tshirts, and what type of tshirts should be made. I am planning on using springtime colors for this project, something light and calm. I will probably be using lato as my typography.
